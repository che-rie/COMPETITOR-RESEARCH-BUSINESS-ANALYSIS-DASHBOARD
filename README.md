# COMPETITOR-RESEARCH-BUSINESS-ANALYSIS-DASHBOARD
Beauty Industry Competitor Research Dashboard

This project involved conducting competitor research and operational analysis within the beauty and body art industry. The dashboard was developed to monitor competitor pricing, service offerings, branding strategies, customer engagement, and market positioning.

The objective was to support business decision-making through organized market research, operational insights, and spreadsheet-based reporting systems.


Tools Used
• Google Sheets
• Excel
• Charts & Visualizations
• Research Documentation

Key Tasks Performed
• Collected competitor operational data
• Compared service pricing structures
• Analyzed customer engagement strategies
• Organized competitor business insights
• Created visual performance tracking dashboards

Findings
• Competitor pricing varied significantly by service category
• Businesses with strong social engagement showed higher visibility
• Branding consistency improved customer perception
• Service bundling increased perceived value

Recommendations
• Improve customer engagement consistency
• Introduce bundled service offers
• Strengthen operational branding visibility
• Monitor competitor pricing regularly
